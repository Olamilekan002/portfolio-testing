About Me

Olamilekan is a Data Scientist and a Microsoft Certified Data Analyst, with an MBA in Business Administration.

With over three years of expertise in the data arena, he has excelled not only as a Data Science instructor and a Machine Learning Engineer but has also taken on roles in analytics and big data projects. His recent endeavours include building NLP solutions and enhancing his portfolio with hands-on experience in Large language model at Turing.

Olamilekan's commitment to continuous learning and professional development is evident in his recent certifications in Data, AI, and tech, further solidifying his position as an emerging leader in the field of data science.

In addition to his impressive background, Olamilekan was one of the coaches at the 3MTT program, where he contributes to the development of upcoming data scientists by sharing his vast knowledge and experience.

He has received notable awards for his contributions to the field and serves as an ambassador for Zindi Africa and Data Science Nigeria, showcasing his dedication to fostering a community of data science professionals in Africa. Through these roles, Olamilekan is not only advancing his career but also actively participating in the growth and development of the data science community on the continent.
